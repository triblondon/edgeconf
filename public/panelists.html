Edge Conference

Panelists

The main purpose of Edge panelists is to help promote discussion, not to own it. There are here as much to learn from you as to share their own experience. We open each hour-long session with a ten minute talk. After that, we're into the discussion, and we keep it moving fast. We record everything on video and in text. Everything is release for free to the world the moment the event ends.
